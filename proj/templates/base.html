Vishnu Institute for Technology University Admission 2023: Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) has announced the exam dates for AP PGECET 2023. The AP PGECET 2023 will be conducted from May 28 to 30, 2023, to offer admission to postgraduate Engineering and Pharmacy courses such as M.E./MTech, M. Pharma, and Pharm. D, in the state of Andhra Pradesh. The registration process will tentatively begin in April 2023. Candidates can check the official website for more details- cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/APSCHEHome.aspx.Candidates who wish to apply for the MBA and MCA courses must appear for the AP ICET 2023 from May 25 - 26, 2023. Admission to the BEd course will be done on the basis of AP EDCET 2023, scheduled on May 20, 2023.

Vishnu Institute for Technology, Bhimavaram was established under the aegis of Sri Vishnu Educational Society in the year 2008. The institute is ranked 149th for B.Tech by IndiaToday 2022 and ranked 106th for B.Tech by The Week 2022. VIT Bhimavaram offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in the discipline of Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering and many more. Admission is based on marks obtained in the qualifying exam. The 2020 batch has witnessed a growth in the placement statistics from 88% (2018) to 97% (2020). The highest salary package offered to students of the 2021 outgoing batch is INR 41 LPA and the lowest salary package is offered at INR 3 LPA. Some of the potential recruiters are Hexaware, Accenture, Amazon, IBM, Hyundai, Infosys, Capgemini, etc.
